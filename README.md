# The Last 60 Days
### An Unquit® Interactive Experience

> *"Move from 'I want to quit' to 'I can take one more step'"*

A narrative decision game based on the **Unquit® Decision Architecture** — a 10-stage framework that moves someone from the edge of quitting to choosing one more step.

## The Story

You are **Elena Rodriguez**, an immigration attorney with 19 years of experience in Houston, Texas. Your client Diego Herrera — a construction foreman with 22 years in America and three US-citizen daughters — has had his appeal denied on a technicality. He has 60 days to leave the country his daughters call home.

You are sitting in your car in a parking garage. The sun is going down. And you are thinking about quitting.

## How It Works

- **12 Levels** mapped to the 10 stages of the Unquit® Decision Architecture
- **A/B branching** — every choice reflects a different internal voice
- **Accumulating resources** — what you choose reveals what you carry
- **Two endings** based on the pattern of your choices
- A **Quit Check** flow if you choose to stop
- A final **Unquit® Declaration** to close the journey
